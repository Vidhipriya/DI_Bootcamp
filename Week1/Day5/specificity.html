Remember the specificity rules with the phrase- inline with ICT ,it illustrates the descending order of importance given to CSS. Inline is given the most priority ,followed by id ,class and tags.

In the same way your identity is unique and there is only one you. ID's(identities) have unique identifiers as well and therefore take unique values.

Contrastingly ,classes are not particularly unique. For instance, there are many classes of HTML and CSS in this world.
